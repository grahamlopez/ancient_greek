Verbs

ἀποφαίνω show, declare (implied by “speak nonsense” φλυαρεῖν – see below) Thematic verb
βοάω shout Contract verb in -άω (βοή, βοᾶν)
ζητέω seek, look for Contract verb in -έω
ἥκει it arrives, has come ἥκω – pres act; intransitive
ἠχεῖ it echoes ἠχέω/ἠχῶ – contract verb in -έω/-ῶ
λέγω say, speak (σύ λέγεις “you say”) Thematic verb
σιγάω be quiet, keep quiet Contract verb in -άω (here imperatives σιγᾶτε, σίγα)
φλυαρέω speak nonsense Contract verb in -έω

Prepositions

ἐν (+ dat.) in, among (implied by “among the barbarians”) Preposition with dative
περὶ (+ gen./acc.) around, about (περὶ τὰ … “around the events”) Preposition with genitive/accusative

Particles and miscellaneous forms
